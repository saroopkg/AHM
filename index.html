AHM Holding

AHM Holding is a privately run group of companies, operating in the fields of real estate, F&B and investments, established in 2014, headquartered in Dubai, UAE.

Mission
AHM pursues its developmental path in accordance to achieve its company mission. The strategy is to achieve sustainable growth whilst maximizing shareholder's equity. It is of particular significance for AHM to maintain a solid capital structure and a strong financial position, in order to ensure stable operational and developmental progression.
Vision
AHM’s vision is to capitalize on its areas of strength and to continue to build upon its solid reputation in those fields. We consider ourselves to be successful in those pursuits, which not only bring us returns, but also those have the most positive impact upon others.

AHM Real Estate

As a leading property developer across UAE and Oman, there is particular focus on schemes with a long-term vision and growth potential. We have leveraged our extensive expertise in the property market to build a varied portfolio of development projects and investment assets. This includes residential, commercial, retail and mixed-use developments. We are renowned for setting new standards in the real estate industry as we develop properties that are both aesthetically attractive and functionally convenient to our customers.

AHM F&B

AHM’s foray into the F&B sector stems from passion in the industry. Our interest lies in designing of unique and feasible dining concepts. Project management from conception till execution lies in the hands of our in-house team, which is highly experienced in this region’s F&B industry. We are continuously investing in talent to grow our presence in the regions we invest in. This has not only been the key recipe behind our innovative concepts, but our focus on this theory makes us the business partner of choice.

AHM Investments

AHM Holding’s investment team is actively involved in allocation of capital into various asset classes. We aim to earn attractive returns for shareholders and investment partners in areas where we see the opportunity to add value. Our focus on key sectors including financial services is driven by robust, secular trends. Our approach is supported by well-defined investment theories, rooted in our macro perspective developed through our experiences across regional markets.
